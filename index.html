About Us

The Vice-Chancellor speech to the Students about being independent.

He addressed the Students on where the Enterpreneurship center is situated in the University campus and it's impact on any student that takes advantage of it.

This Center is situated behind the Science Laboratory of College of Natural and Applied Sciences. It is compulsory for each students to choose any skills of their choice because it is going to contribute to the CGPA of such student at the end of his/her Programme. He further mentioned what it entails to be a graduate at Summit University, Offa:

  • A graduating Student must be have completed his four years program.
  • He/She must be found worthy in Character and Knowledge.
  • He/She must be an entrepreneurer as this constitutes to the Tripple Helix.

Why choose us?

The possible services you are waiting to receive from us.

Summit University is hoping to provide a graduate that will not be a job seekers but Job earners.

Many graduates are outside looking for job, though there are jobs out there but very few compare to the numbers of graduates per years. Thus, being an employment of Labour is very helpful because the graduate can look for money to set-up a business and start-up a good life. Therefore, the Entrepreneur Center was created to aid and bring solution of seeking jobs here and there after your programs.
